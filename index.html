About This Project

WHY THE DEEP OCEAN MATTERS

The deep ocean covers more than 65% of Earth's surface and remains more poorly mapped than the surface of Mars. It regulates our climate, produces oxygen, and harbours millions of species yet to be discovered.

Depths Unknown is a tribute to this hidden world — built to inspire curiosity about the planet's last great unexplored frontier.
